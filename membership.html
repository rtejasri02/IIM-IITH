IIM Membership Categories for Students

Student Member: A candidate for admission into student member category, shall be a person, who is being educated or being trained for and who intends to adopt a profession in engineering [any specializations], scientific research, academics/ related fields . Student members will automatically become Associate Members as soon as they acquire necessary qualifications

Eligibility Criteria : Passed Higher Secondary (10+2) or equivalent with Physics Chemistry and Mathematics with at least 2 years of Industrial Experience. OR B.Sc. with any subject combination after passing Higher Secondary (10+2) or equivalent with Physics Chemistry and Mathematics. OR Holding Diploma in Engineering after passing Higher Secondary (10+2) or equivalent with Physics Chemistry and Mathematics.
